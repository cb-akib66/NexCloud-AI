Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about NexCloud AI.

What is NexCloud AI and what do you do?
+
NexCloud AI is a Bangladesh-based cloud and AI services company. We help businesses set up AWS infrastructure, automate workflows using AI, build smart chatbots, and provide AI training and consulting.
How long does it take to set up my cloud infrastructure?
+
Basic AWS setups typically take 3–7 business days. Complex architectures can take 2–4 weeks. We provide daily updates throughout the process.
Can you build an AI chatbot for my business?
+
Absolutely! We build AI-powered chatbots for WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and websites. Most chatbots are delivered within 5–10 business days.
Do I need technical knowledge to work with NexCloud AI?
+
Not at all! We handle all the technical complexity. You just tell us your business needs and goals, and we translate them into technical solutions.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept bKash, Nagad, bank transfer, and international payments via Payoneer or Wise. 50% advance, 50% on project completion.
